FLIP PAY CARD SPECIFICATIONS

Hardware Specifications

1. Form factor: credit card size 85.6(L) x 53.98(H) x0.8(T)mm
2. Display: Eink e---paper; segmented; black white color; contrast ratio 7:1; ~180 viewangle; 24.6(L) x 21.9(H)mm view area; plastic material.
3. Button: 1 metal dome button; >100,000 pushes; tactile feedback.
4. Battery: 1 lithium ion rechargeable battery; 3V; 10mAh; 500 recharging cycles and two years battery life.
5. Battery charging
- NFC: 1 hour from 20% to 85%, 4 hours full charge
6. Communication interface
- Bluetooth: BLE 4.0; 10 meters proximity distance
- NFC: 0~1cm proximity distance
7. Power consumption: Min 30 days for standby or 30 transactions.
8. Working temperature: 10C—40C
9. Storagetemperature: (---)10C—50C
10. Secure Chip: Common Criteria EAL5+ certified
- Temper proof
- True random number generator
- Hardware Crypto Accelerator

Supported Software

1. FLIP PAY Cold Wallet iPhone App via BLE communication
2. FLIP PAY Cold Wallet Android App via BLE communication

Functions

1. FLIP specification supported:
- FLIP algorithm: Support transaction signing algorithm ECDSA with specified elliptic curve secp256k1
- BIP---32: HD Wallets
- BIP---44: Support upto 5 accounts,each account includes upto 2^32 external and internal FLIP transaction address.
2. Generate seed on---card or import via communication interface.
3. Strong authentication and secure---channel with wallet applications.
4. OTP algorithm: OATH HOTP
5. Watchdog: Alert when lost signal
